🎯 **Objective:** Use GHL's reputation tools to collect, manage, and respond to online reviews — building trust and attracting more customers.

---

## 8.1 Why Online Reviews Matter

| Stat | Impact |
|------|--------|
| **93%** of consumers | Read online reviews before making a purchase |
| **84%** of people | Trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations |
| **4+ stars** | Businesses with 4+ stars get 12× more leads than 1-2 star businesses |
| **70%** of consumers | Will leave a review if asked |

> The bottom line: **Reviews = Revenue.** GHL automates the process of asking happy customers for reviews and managing your online reputation.

## 8.3 Setting Up Reputation Management

### Step 1: Connect Review Platforms
1. Go to **Reputation** in the sidebar
2. Connect **Google Business Profile** (formerly Google My Business)
3. Connect **Facebook Page**

### Step 2: Create a Review Request Campaign

**The Smart Review Funnel:**

```
Customer completes a service/purchase
    │
    ▼
Workflow sends SMS or Email:
"How was your experience? Rate us 1–5"
    │
    ├── [Rating 4-5 ⭐]
    │       ▼
    │   "Glad you loved it! Would you mind leaving us a Google review?"
    │   [Link to Google Review page]
    │
    └── [Rating 1-3 ⭐]
            ▼
        "We're sorry to hear that. What can we improve?"
        [Link to private feedback form]
        → Notify manager for follow-up
```

### Step 3: Automate via Workflow

```
Trigger: Invoice Paid / Appointment Completed / Tag "service_completed"
    │
    ▼
[Wait 1 hour]
    SMS: "Hi {{first_name}}, how was your experience today? Reply 1-5"
    │
    ├── [Reply 4 or 5]
    │       Email: "Thank you! 🌟 We'd love a quick Google review"
    │       + Direct link to Google review
    │
    ├── [Reply 1-3]
    │       SMS: "We're sorry. Can you tell us what went wrong?"
    │       → Create Task: "Manager: Call {{first_name}} to resolve issue"
    │
    └── [No reply in 2 days]
            SMS: "Hi {{first_name}}, we'd love your feedback! Quick 1-5 rating?"
```

## 8.4 Responding to Reviews

### Response Templates

**Positive Review (5 stars):**
> Thank you so much, [Name]! We really appreciate your kind words. It was a pleasure working with you, and we look forward to helping you again soon! ⭐

**Neutral Review (3 stars):**
> Thank you for your feedback, [Name]. We're glad you had a good experience, and we'd love to know how we can earn that 5th star. Please reach out to us at [email/phone] — we'd love to hear your suggestions.

**Negative Review (1-2 stars):**
> We're sorry to hear about your experience, [Name]. This isn't the standard we aim for. Please contact us directly at [email/phone] so we can make it right. Your satisfaction is our top priority.

### Response Best Practices
1. **Respond within 24 hours** — speed matters
2. **Always be professional** — never argue publicly
3. **Personalize** — use the reviewer's name
4. **Take it offline** — offer a phone call or email for resolution
5. **Thank everyone** — even negative reviewers

## 8.5 Reputation Dashboard & Reporting

The dashboard shows:
- **Overall rating** (average across platforms)
- **Review volume** (total and trend over time)
- **Recent reviews** (newest first, with reply status)
- **Platform breakdown** (Google vs. Facebook ratings)
- **Response rate** (% of reviews responded to)

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| Average rating | > 4.5 stars | Higher ratings = more clicks |
| Monthly review volume | 5–10+ new reviews/month | Fresh reviews boost SEO |
| Response rate | 100% | Shows prospects you care |
| Negative review % | < 5% | Indicates service quality |
