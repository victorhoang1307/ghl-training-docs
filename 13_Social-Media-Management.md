## 13.4 Creating & Scheduling Posts

### 13.4.1 Creating a New Post

1. Click **"Create Post"**
2. Select target **platforms** (multi-select)
3. Write your **caption/content**
   - Use `AI Assistant` (✨) to generate or improve copy
   - Add **hashtags** relevant to each platform
4. Attach **media**: images, videos, or GIFs
   - Built-in image editor for quick adjustments
   - Watermark option for brand protection
5. Choose **timing**:
   - **Post Now** — publish immediately
   - **Schedule** — set a specific date and time
   - **Add to Queue** — auto-post based on your queue schedule
6. **Preview** how the post will look on each platform
7. Click **Schedule** or **Publish**

### 13.4.2 Bulk Content Upload

For agencies managing multiple accounts or planning weeks ahead:
1. Prepare content in a **CSV file** (date, time, caption, media URL, platform)
2. Go to Social Planner → **Bulk Upload**
3. Map CSV columns → import
4. Review and adjust in the calendar view

### 13.4.3 Content Categories & Queues

Organize posts into categories for automated rotation:

```
Category: "Tips & Tricks"     → Post every Monday 10 AM
Category: "Customer Stories"  → Post every Wednesday 2 PM
Category: "Behind the Scenes" → Post every Friday 11 AM
Category: "Promotions"        → Post every Saturday 9 AM
```

Posts added to a category queue automatically publish at the next available slot.

## 13.8 Social Media Analytics

Track performance across all connected platforms:

| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|------------------|
| **Reach** | How many unique people saw your post |
| **Impressions** | Total number of times your post was displayed |
| **Engagement** | Likes, comments, shares, saves |
| **Engagement Rate** | Engagement ÷ Reach |
| **Clicks** | Link clicks from the post |
| **Follower Growth** | Net new followers over time |

### Reporting Tips
1. Review analytics **weekly** — identify top-performing content
2. Double down on content types with **high engagement rates**
3. Post at times when your audience is **most active**
4. Compare performance **across platforms** to allocate effort wisely
