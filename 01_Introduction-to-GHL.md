# Module 1: Introduction to GoHighLevel (GHL)

рҹҺҜ **Objective:** Understand the GHL platform at a high level вҖ” what it does, why it matters, and how it replaces multiple tools for Sales & Marketing.

---

## 1.1 What is GoHighLevel?

GoHighLevel (GHL) is an **all-in-one platform** for businesses and agencies that consolidates every essential Sales & Marketing tool into a single, unified dashboard.

### The Problem Before GHL


> рҹ’Ў **Key Benefit:** One login, one bill, and all your data flows seamlessly between modules вҖ” no more copy-pasting between apps.

## 1.3 Navigating the Dashboard


When you log in, the **left sidebar** is your main navigation:

1. **Dashboard** вҖ” KPIs and business overview
2. **Conversations** вҖ” Unified Inbox (all channels in one place)
3. **Calendars** вҖ” Appointment management
4. **Contacts** вҖ” CRM & contact records
5. **Opportunities** вҖ” Sales Pipelines
6. **Payments** вҖ” Invoices & transactions
7. **Marketing** вҖ” Email, SMS, Social Planner, Ad Manager
8. **Automation** вҖ” Workflows
9. **Sites** вҖ” Funnels, Websites, Landing Pages
10. **Memberships** вҖ” Courses, Communities, Client Portal
11. **Reputation** вҖ” Review management
12. **Reporting** вҖ” Reports & Analytics
13. **Advertising** вҖ” Facebook & Google Ads management
14. **Settings** вҖ” System configuration

## 1.5 Key Terminology

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Sub-Account** | A separate workspace for each client or business unit |
| **Contact** | A lead or customer record in the CRM |
| **Opportunity** | A deal attached to a pipeline stage |
| **Pipeline** | A visual sales process with defined stages |
| **Workflow** | A sequence of automated actions |
| **Trigger** | An event that starts a workflow |
| **Funnel** | A multi-step sales sequence (landing pages + forms) |
| **Smart List** | A dynamic contact list that auto-updates based on filters |
| **Tag** | A label attached to a contact for segmentation |
| **Custom Field** | A user-defined data field on a contact record |
| **Snapshot** | A pre-built template that imports an entire account configuration |
| **Social Planner** | Tool for scheduling and publishing posts across social media platforms |
| **Ad Manager** | Centralized dashboard for running Facebook & Google Ads |
| **Membership** | Gated access to courses, content, or communities |
| **Community** | Branded online forum for members to interact and share |
| **Course** | An online learning module with lessons, quizzes, and certificates |
| **ROAS** | Return on Ad Spend вҖ” revenue generated per dollar spent on ads |
