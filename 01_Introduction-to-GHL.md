## 1.1 What is GoHighLevel?

GoHighLevel (GHL) is an **all-in-one platform** for businesses and agencies that consolidates every essential Sales & Marketing tool into a single, unified dashboard.

### The Problem Before GHL

Most teams juggle multiple tools — and pay for each one separately:

| CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce) | $50 – $300 | ✅ Built-in CRM |
| Email Marketing (Mailchimp) | $30 – $150 | ✅ Email Builder |
| SMS Marketing (Twilio) | $20 – $100 | ✅ SMS Built-in |
| Funnel Builder (ClickFunnels) | $97 – $297 | ✅ Funnel Builder |
| Scheduling (Calendly) | $10 – $15 | ✅ Calendars |
| Reviews (BirdEye) | $300+ | ✅ Reputation Mgmt |
| **Total** | **$500 – $1,200+** | **$97 – $497** |

> 💡 **Key Benefit:** One login, one bill, and all your data flows seamlessly between modules — no more copy-pasting between apps.

## 1.4 Navigating the Dashboard

When you log in, the **left sidebar** is your main navigation:

1. **Dashboard** — KPIs and business overview
2. **Conversations** — Unified Inbox (all channels in one place)
3. **Calendars** — Appointment management
4. **Contacts** — CRM & contact records
5. **Opportunities** — Sales Pipelines
6. **Payments** — Invoices & transactions
7. **Marketing** — Email, SMS, Social Planner
8. **Automation** — Workflows
9. **Sites** — Funnels, Websites, Landing Pages
10. **Reputation** — Review management
11. **Reporting** — Reports & Analytics
12. **Settings** — System configuration

## 1.5 Key Terminology

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Sub-Account** | A separate workspace for each client or business unit |
| **Contact** | A lead or customer record in the CRM |
| **Opportunity** | A deal attached to a pipeline stage |
| **Pipeline** | A visual sales process with defined stages |
| **Workflow** | A sequence of automated actions |
| **Trigger** | An event that starts a workflow |
| **Funnel** | A multi-step sales sequence (landing pages + forms) |
| **Smart List** | A dynamic contact list that auto-updates based on filters |
| **Tag** | A label attached to a contact for segmentation |
| **Custom Field** | A user-defined data field on a contact record |
| **Snapshot** | A pre-built template that imports an entire account configuration |
